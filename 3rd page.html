Schooling

Georgian college and Lakehead university offer a 4 year program with 2 years with each school it is located in barrie and Orillia, Ontario, Canada. The admission requirements are English(ENG4U), Advanced Functions(MHF4U), one credit from: calculus and vectors(MCV4U), Data management(MDM4U), or computer science(ICS4U). A minimum final average, based on the best six grade 12 U or M courses, of 70% is required. There is 4 years of this course 2 at Georgian college and 2 at Lakehead university. This course costs $6,797.54 for domestic fees and $27,313 for international fees.
